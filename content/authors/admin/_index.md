I am a **Ramanujan Faculty Fellow** at **IIT Kanpur**, specializing in the interdisciplinary frontier of Non-Volatile Memory (RRAM) and Neuromorphic Computing. 

My work is driven by two goals:
1. **Academic Innovation:** Advancing storage-class memory and energy-efficient hardware for Edge-AI.
2. **Industrial Impact:** Developing scalable nanofabrication processes (CVD, ALD, Lithography) that align with the **India Semiconductor Mission**.

With over 35 publications in journals like *Advanced Functional Materials* and *Carbon*, and secured funding of over ₹2.5 Cr, I am building a "Semiconductor-to-Materials-to-Device" ecosystem at IIT Kanpur.
